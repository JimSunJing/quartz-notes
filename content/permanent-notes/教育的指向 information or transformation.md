教育可以分为 information 和 transformation. Information 只完成了脑海中知识的堆积，transformation 会因为知识的进入改变容器的形状（人的观念）；transformation 之后，人对事物的看法会变得不同。 “It is not just the information that goes in it’s also the shape, the interpretation, the perception, the focus, and that is determined by the shape of the form”
